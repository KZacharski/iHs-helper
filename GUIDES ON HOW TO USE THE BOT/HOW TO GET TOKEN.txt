what is the token?
it's a string of letters and numbers required to log into discord

how do i get my bot's token?
go to discord.com/developers/applications
login to discord
press new application (in top right corner)
give the bot a nice name
enter bot section (on left side)
press add bot (top right corner)
press copy under token (under the username of your bot)

REMEMBER: NEVER GIVE ANYONE YOUR TOKEN!

now your bot's token is in your clipboard
